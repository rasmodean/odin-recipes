Oatmeal

This is plant based oatmeal, full of micro-nutrients and fiber

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Oatmeal
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Bananna
  • Rasins
  • Walnuts
  • Almonds
  • Tripple Berry Medley
  • Cinnimon
    • 1

Steps

  1. Mash bananna in bowl
  2. Pour oatmeal and water into bowl
  3. Cook oatmeal
  4. Add rasins, walnuts, almonds, frozen berries, and Cinnimon
